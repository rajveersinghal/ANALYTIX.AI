# AnalytixAI: The Ultimate Guide to Data Intelligence

Welcome to the official documentation for **AnalytixAI**. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of how to use the platform, a deep dive into our 11-step AI pipeline, and instructions for managing your data workspaces.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Navigate to the [Landing Page](/) and click **"Start Free Analysis"** or **"Sign In"**.
2. If you don't have an account, switch to the **"Sign Up"** tab.
3. Once registered, you will be automatically placed into the **Free Tier**, allowing you 1 full autonomous analysis per 24 hours.

### Your First Workspace
AnalytixAI uses a **Workspace-first** approach. Before uploading data, you must create a project container:
1. Go to the **Workspaces** (Projects) dashboard.
2. Click **"New Workspace"**.
3. Name your project (e.g., "Q4 Sales Performance" or "Customer Churn Analysis").
4. Click on the card to enter the workspace.

## 2. The 11-Step Autonomous Pipeline

When you drop a file into AnalytixAI, our engine performs a high-speed, 11-stage intelligence cycle:

| Step | Phase | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **01** | **Ingestion** | Parses CSV/Excel into memory-safe data structures. |
| **02** | **Metadata Mining** | Identifies data types, missing values, and statistical distributions. |
| **03** | **Target Inference** | Automatically identifies the "Goal" (e.g., predicting 'Revenue'). |
| **04** | **Auto-Cleaning** | Handles missing data using advanced statistical imputation (Mean/Median). |
| **05** | **Feature Engineering** | Scales variables and creates interaction terms to improve model accuracy. |
| **06** | **Clustering/EDA** | Groups your data to find hidden segments and correlations. |
| **07** | **AutoML Racing** | Trains multiple models (XGBoost, Random Forest, Linear) simultaneously. |
| **08** | **Model Selection** | Promotes the most accurate model based on F1-Score and R2. |
| **09** | **XAI (Explainability)** | Uses **SHAP** values to tell you *exactly* which factors drove your results. |
| **10** | **LLM Synthesis** | Google Gemini translates complex stats into a human strategic narrative. |
| **11** | **Deployment** | Your dashboard is hydrated with interactive charts and actionable playbooks. |

## 3. Core Features & Tools

### 🔮 Predictive Forecasting
AnalytixAI doesn't just look at the past. Our models forecast future trends based on historical curves. You'll see "Predicted vs Actual" charts that help you plan inventory and staffing.

### 📉 SHAP Explainability
Stop guessing why sales dropped. Our **Explainability Engine** uses game theory (SHAP) to highlight the "Drivers" of your data. 
*   *Example:* "Region: South and SKU: Electronics are responsible for 74% of the revenue growth."

### 💬 Contextual AI Chat (Gemini)
In the bottom-right of your dashboard, you'll find an **AI Chatbot**. 
*   It is "Session-Aware," meaning it knows exactly which dataset you are looking at.
*   Ask: "Explain the Q3 dip" or "Which products should I discontinue?" for instant data-backed answers.

### 💳 Tier Management
*   **Free**: 1 analysis/day, Standard models, PDF Export.
*   **Pro (₹499/mo)**: Unlimited analyses, Advanced XGBoost, Priority Gemini LLM, SHAP Deep-Dives.
*   **Enterprise**: Custom ERP integrations (Shopify, WooCommerce, SQL).

## 4. Privacy & Security
*   **Zero Retention**: We process your data in RAM. Raw datasets are purged instantly after the pipeline finishes.
*   **Encryption**: All transmissions are secured via TLS/SSL.
*   **Isolation**: Your data is logically isolated. No other user can ever access your workspaces or models.

## 5. Troubleshooting & Support
*   **File Format**: Ensure your file is a valid `.csv` or `.xlsx`. 
*   **Max Size**: 25MB (Free Tier) / 100MB (Pro Tier).
*   **Target Column**: If the AI picks the wrong target, you can manually select it before starting the training.
