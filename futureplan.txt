AnalytixAI is currently in a **High-Fidelity MVP (Minimum Viable Product)** state. For a seed-stage startup, this is a very complete and impressive product—it goes far beyond a basic prototype by offering autonomous 11-step pipelines, specialized sales intelligence, and a live "What-If" simulator.

However, to move from a **Basic Startup Product** to a **Scalable Enterprise SaaS**, I recommend the following three focused improvements for your next phase of development:

### 1. Data Connectivity (The "No-Manual-Entry" Move)
*   **Current State**: Manual CSV/Excel uploads only.
*   **Target State**: Add "Connectors." High-value users don't want to export files; they want to link their **PostgreSQL**, **Snowflake**, or **Google Sheets** directly.
*   **Benefit**: This turns the product from a "tool" into a "persistent dashboard" that updates as their data grows.

### 2. Team Collaboration (The "Sticky User" Move)
*   **Current State**: Single-user workspaces.
*   **Target State**: Shared Workspaces. Allow a user to invite a "Viewer" or "Editor" via email. 
*   **Benefit**: SaaS growth is driven by word-of-mouth. If a manager can share a result with their team, the product becomes essential for decision-making across the whole company.

### 3. Stripe & Usage Enforcement (The "Monetization" Move)
*   **Current State**: We have a Pricing/Billing UI, but the logic is largely illustrative.
*   **Target State**: Actual backend enforcement. If a user is on the "Free" plan and tries to upload their 6th dataset, the system should block it and trigger a high-conversion upgrade popup.
*   **Benefit**: This validates that people are willing to pay for your intelligence.
